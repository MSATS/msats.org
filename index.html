Welcome to the
Municipal Sewage Authority of Sewickly Township

The sewage authority services approximately 1300 customers located in the Herminie, Rillton, Cowansburg, Sunshine and Keystone areas of Sewickley Township, Westmoreland County. On our website, you can view important documents and updates, download forms, contact us, and even pay your bill.

Online Bill Payment

Online bill payment can be accessed at https://msats.secure.munibilling.com

You will need your account number, the last name on the account, and your payment method to bring your account balance up-to-date.
